Graphic Design

More Details:

In my own time I enjoy creating mixed media prints that incorperate both graphic design practices as well as in person artistic practices to create visually appealing images in a collage style. The images displayed are collage pieces made over the past 3 years that make use of the aforementioned artistic practices and were excercies for me personally to be able to create meaningfull works of art that followed specific themes.

Matric Jacket

More Details:

In 11th grade I volunteered to design the matric jacket that my grade would go on to use the following year. The images displayed are photos of the jackets design sketch, as well as photos of the jacket itself.

Pixel Man

More Details:

Pixel Man is a 3D video game I developed in my first semester of third year game design. The game follows a stickman that gets stuck in an arcade game and has to defeat the enemies in the game to escape. I developed this game individually and all the 3D models seen in the gameplay provided was created by myself.
